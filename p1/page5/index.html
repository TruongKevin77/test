Controls:

Example PS4 controller setup:

  • Left analog stick is used for character movement.
  • Right analog stick is used for camera controls.
  • Triangle is used for primary attack.
  • Circle is used for secondary attack.
  • X is used for rolling.
  • Square is used for consuming items.
  • Right bumper is used for sprinting.
  • D-pad is also used for camera controls.
  • Left bumper is used for locking onto a beast or cycling through items in inventory.
